Info

Pax South is a gaming convention held in San Antonio, Texas on a weekend January 18-20th 2019 where many streamers and streaming community members come together to have fun. It is the easiest con to attend for the most people in our community.
Official information can be found at official website and on Twitter at Pax 1 Pax 2.

This website exists to give basic meetup information for Pax South attendees for the smaller to mid streaming community to network and build helpful relationships with each other. If we are more organized before hand, we can spend more fun time with each other at the event and less time in confusion, in lines, finding who is there, etc. You can find conventions where "Ninja" or "Markiplier" is attending and you get to stand in line to shake their hand -- this is for you and every gaming streaming like you to meet together and talk about the issues about live streaming concerning you. And have some fun afterwards!

A majority of the community stays at the Marriott Riverwalk hotel. It is across the street from the convention and part of a mall building that holds many restaurants/bars inside of it. You will not need to rent a car there, everything is within a block or two.

The hotel and the convention are easily accesible for those who have difficulty walking or in wheelchairs: these people are at the forefront of our planning thoughts.

Planning for the meetup this year is being helped by 3 residents of San Antonio, several residents of Texas among others. Boots are on the ground already to make sure you have a great time!

Travel

Travel to Pax South is easy.
1) Those living in Texas can probably drive to San Antonio.
2) Those outside of Texas can fly in to San Antonio airport. Then it is a 15 minute drive to the convention/hotel! You can probably even get a ride from the airport from someone you know from the streams.
3) It is easy to walk around downtown from there!

Most flights in the USA to Pax South when purchased a couple months out are $200 - $500. Its really not that bad, if you save $50 a month you will be there in no time. Con tickets are around $40 a day, and you don't even have to attend the con to meet people if you don't want to spend the money.

International flights from South America and Europe should try to fly into Atlanta USA airport, then connect to San Antonio for the con. It is only a 3 hour flight from Atlanta to San Antonio.

There are Amtrak train lines to San Antonio if you don't want to fly.

Weather in the northern USA may be rough at that time, but weather for travel in San Antonio should be good -- sunny and warm.

Flying is pretty easy these days, known at least a half dozen people who took their first flight to Pax and enjoyed the trip rather than having horror stories about it.

New for this year in 2019 is the music meetup or streamer jam session are trying to organize. In addition to eating, drinking, playing video games and chatting, there are some streamers and community members attending which may write and perform music to their own channel and have an interest in video game music or playing music on the side.

We are only trying to see who be interested in attending a single music meetup at a specific time to have fun playing music together or talking about making/playing music works in live streaming for a couple hours. Or listening to your favourite streamers perform together.

The event would be free and not cost money.
